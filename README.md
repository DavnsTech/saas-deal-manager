# Deal Manager

Deal Manager is a comprehensive CRM designed to streamline B2B and B2C sales processes for teams of up to 10 people. It provides a clear overview of deals, manages sales stages, and offers customization to fit your specific business needs.

## Features

*   **Deal Management:** Track deals from prospection to closing and beyond.
*   **Sales Stage Visualization:** Monitor deals across defined sales stages.
*   **Customizable Fields:** Adapt the CRM with custom fields relevant to your business.
*   **Intuitive Dashboard:** Get a quick overview of your sales performance.
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** Navigate easily through deals and client information.
